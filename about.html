About ME!
Hi! I am Marios Rafail Stinis a full-time illustrator and part-time pencil hoarder. I specialize in bringing characters to life, whether they’re goofy, grumpy, magical, or wearing way too many hats (literally). From children’s books to comics and quirky character designs, I love turning ideas into eye-catching visuals that make people smile, gasp, or say “aww.”

I can help with storytelling, character design, book illustration, and turning weird doodles into polished magic. Basically, if it needs a little more charm, I’m your artist.

Let’s make something fun together!
